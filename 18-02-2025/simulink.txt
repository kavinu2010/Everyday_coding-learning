what is simulink?
It s a simulation and model- based design environment for modelling, 
simulation and analysis of multidomain systems, in graphical way

Simulink is a graphical programming environment integrated with MATLAB, 
developed by MathWorks, used for modeling, simulating, and analyzing dynamic systems.
